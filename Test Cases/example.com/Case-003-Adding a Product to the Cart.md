# Adding a Product to the Cart

## Description:
Successfully adding a product to the cart by the user

## Pre-conditions:
• The user is authorized

• At least one product is added to the wishlist

• The user is on the wishlist page

## Post-conditions:
• The website remains on the same page

• The user’s cart is displayed

## Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open the website: https://www.example.com/
2. Click any product added to the wishlist
3. Click the "კალათაში დამატება" button

## Expected result:
• The button is clicked successfully

• The cart and the added product appear on the right side of the website
