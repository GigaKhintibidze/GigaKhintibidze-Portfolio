# 🐞 Notifications for “New reactions to your rebuts” are not received despite being enabled

## Environment
**Test Device:** iPhone 11

**Operating System:** iOS 26.1

**Application Version:** Version 1.0.0 (1.0.52)

**Reproducibility Rate:** 100%

## Pre-Conditions:
• User is logged in

• “New reactions to your rebuts” notification is enabled in Notification Settings

• User has published a debate on the For You page

• The debate has at least one reaction

## Steps to Reproduce:

1. Open the mobile application: Rebut
2. Tap the Notification button located at the bottom

## Actual Result:
No notification is displayed in the Notifications tab regarding new reactions on the debate, even though “New reactions to your rebuts” is enabled in Notification Settings.

## Expected Result:
If “New reactions to your rebuts” is enabled in Notification Settings, the system should generate a notification for each new reaction and display it in the Notifications tab.
